Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23 February 2026

VRC (Voice Remote Controller) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the VRC mobile application handles information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

VRC does not collect, store, or share personal information such as name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or media files. The app does not require account registration or login.

Wi-Fi Credentials

During device setup, the app may request Wi-Fi SSID and password to configure your VRC hardware device. This information is transmitted locally to your device and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability. Crash reports may include device model, operating system version, app version, and technical diagnostic information. This data does not personally identify users and is used only to fix bugs and improve performance.

2. Data Storage

The app operates locally between your smartphone and your VRC hardware device. We do not maintain backend servers that store user data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute user data. We do not use advertising SDKs or behavioral tracking technologies.

4. Children's Privacy

VRC does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data transmitted within your local network.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
